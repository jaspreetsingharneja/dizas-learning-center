📚 What We Teach

Our Programs

Focused, results-driven coaching for elementary (K–5) and middle school (6–8) students.

🔢

Mathematics

From counting and fractions to algebra and geometry — we make numbers make sense with a step-by-step, confidence-building approach.

Elementary (K–5) Middle School (6–8)
  • Number sense & arithmetic
  • Fractions, decimals & percentages
  • Pre-Algebra & Algebra basics
  • Geometry fundamentals
🔬

Science

Curiosity is our classroom! We explore the natural world through experiments, visuals, and hands-on activities that bring science alive.

Elementary (K–5) Middle School (6–8)
  • Life, Earth & Physical science
  • Simple experiments at home
  • Scientific method basics
  • Biology & Chemistry intro
📖

English & Reading

Strong readers become strong thinkers. We nurture a love of language through phonics, comprehension, writing, and creative storytelling.

Elementary (K–5) Middle School (6–8)
  • Phonics & reading fluency
  • Reading comprehension
  • Vocabulary building
  • Writing & grammar skills
